Bike Rentals¶

Jathin Danthala¶

Introduction¶

The purpose of this project is to analyze bicycle rental data to see when people rent bicycles the most and why. The data set provides information about the weather, time of day, and whether the day was a holiday or not. We can see from these observations the effects of temperature, wind, and even the time of year on the behavior of bicycle renting. This sort of analysis is helpful to the company because it can tell them when to expect more riders and how to plan accordingly. In general, the goal is to find simple patterns in the data for how and when people are using the bikes.

This exploration of bike rentals revealed useful information on when and why the shared bikes are rented. We observed peak demand for rental during commute times, especially at 8 AM and 5 PM. From these findings, the company can improve service planning, predict demand, and promote bike use in off-peak hours. These findings are based on real trends in the data and can be used to make better decisions in the future.
